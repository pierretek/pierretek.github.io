About Me

Hi, I'm Pierre, a third year university student studying computer engineering.

I am passionate about firmware and embedded systems because I love seeing the real life applications of my programs. High-level abstract software always seemed pointless to me because it feels too math heavy with little real world application.

Java was the first language I learned and I love it's object oriented structure and intuitive libraries and functions. Over the years I have dabbled in many languages such as C, Assembly, MATLAB, VHDL, python and several others. Currently, I am studying C++ in depth because I enjoy its wide range of applications and its similarity to C.

Some of my hobbies include chess, table tennis, biking, and taking long walks through new places.

If youve read this far, I thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and I hope you have a great day.
